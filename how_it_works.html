How it works

Prepare inventory

Our experts prepare each VR glasses for use, treating the devices with a special care product after each session.

Choose a movie

In our archive there are educational films on the subject of space, astrology and social science. We work with the largest manufacturer of multimedia products in the Russian Federation.

Put on VR glasses

Pupils get VR glasses, our technician helps them figure out how to put on glasses correctly and fix them on their heads. For children with vision other than 100%, the technician adjusts the diopter.

Explore the galaxies

Children plunge into virtual worlds and enjoy learning everything around. After the show, vivid emotions remain and important moments about planets, stars and galaxies are remembered.
