What is VR-planetarium?

This is an educational program using virtual reality technology.

Spectators are given virtual reality glasses, the operator starts a planetarium session on all glasses at the same time. The film collection includes educational pictures about space, astronomy and social science.

Due to the reality and imitation of the game, such training increases motivation and stimulates the child's active brain activity.
